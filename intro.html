Super Bowl Ads 2000-2020

Vaibhav Karanam, CS-416(Summer 2022), UIUC-MCS

This visualization showcases the Super Bowl ads run by 10 brands from 2000 to 2020. These ads are tagged with 4 different attributes: Danger, Funny, Patriotic, and Sexual. I have used this dataset to analyze what attributes brands constantly use for the last two decades and how these have changed over the years

Brands: Bud Light, Budweiser, Coca-Cola, Doritos, E-Trade, Hyundai, Kia, NFL, Pepsi, Toyota

The visualization contains slide show and comparison of ads by different brands of same industry
