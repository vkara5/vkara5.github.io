Service

CARPOOL

Through this organization, I was able to meet some of the most motivated and ambitious people in my life. The goal of this organization is to facilitate a safe and reliable commuting environment in the Bryan/College Station area by providing free, non-judgmental rides home to intoxicated patrons and/or patrons rendered incapable of transportation due to other circumstances. I joined as a member in the Spring of 2019. I quickly fell in love with CARPOOL's mission and felt extremely welcomed by the members. I took on the position of Deputy Director of Public Relations to take my commitment to this organization further. Through this position, I was able to spread awareness about the work CARPOOL does for the community and the positive impact it has made throughout the years. I am delighted to have been part of this organization.

12th Can

The 12th CAN is a student-run food pantry at Texas A&M University. This organization is very dear to me. When I was growing up in India, I got to witness the extreme poverty around me. It was heart-wrenching to see children going hungry to bed and struggling to get even a single meal throughout the day. I always wanted to make a difference in the lives of those families. I learned about this organization through a friend and immediately loved what they were doing in the community. I am honored to have worked with this organization as I felt like I was making difference in the lives of the people.

The Big Event

The Big Event embodies of what it means to be an Aggie. The idea of selfless service runs deep at Texas A&M and The Big Event is the perfect tradition which showcases this aspect. This one day event provides students the opportunity to give back to the Bryan/College Station community. I have taken part in this event for the last three years.
