Super Bowl Ads

Vaibhav Karanam, CS-416(Summer 2022), UIUC MCS

This slide show visualization showcases the Super Bowl ads run by 10 brands from 2000 to 2020. These ads are tagged with 4 different characteristics/emotions: Danger, Funny, Patriotic, and Sexual. I have used this dataset to analyze what emotions brands constantly use for the last two decades and how these emotions have changed over the years.

Note: An ad can be classified with multiple emotions
